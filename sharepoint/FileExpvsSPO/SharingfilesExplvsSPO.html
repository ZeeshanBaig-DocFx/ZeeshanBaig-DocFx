Sharing Files

Sharing a file in explorer can be done through either email, app or through the local network.

When you share files and folders in SharePoint you can decide whether to let people edit or just view them. You can see who a SharePoint file is shared with, and stop sharing SharePoint files or folders, or change permissions, at any time.

There are options to share items with lots of people you might not even know personally. For example, you can use these links to post to Facebook, Twitter, or LinkedIn, or share in an email or message.
